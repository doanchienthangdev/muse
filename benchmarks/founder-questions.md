# Real Founder Questions

Harvested from public sources. Sources: YC office hours, Indie Hackers forums, Hacker News threads, r/startups discussions, Twitter/X founder Qs, Stratechery comments.

Each prompt is lightly paraphrased to anonymize but preserve the structure of the actual question.
